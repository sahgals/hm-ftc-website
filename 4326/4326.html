-Hardware-

We design and build the robot. We build the robot's skeleton and work outward from there. Through many discussions, 3D modeling and many hours in the machine shop, we are soley responsible for the look, feel, and execution of the robot!

computer

*Software*

We code and test the robot. Through the marvelous world of java coding and the help of the IDE Android Studio, we make the robot move, as well as customize its functionality for the drive team. While hardware assembles the body of the robot, we create its ~soul~!

group

•Public Relations•

We manage outreach and team opportunities. We spread the word of our team's marvelous accomplishments and contributions, in addition to adding our own 4326 flair to everything we do. While software creates the soul of the robot, we shape the soul of the team!

Who We Are

We are the 4326 Horace Mann Basement Lions. We are our school's veteran FTC team, consisting of sophomores and juniors from all over the Tri-State Area. We meet on Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays and Sundays, working as a team for hours at a time to perfect our robot. We compete in New York City, Long Island, New Jersey and Pennsylvania, and support our school and greater Bronx community through our many outreach endeavors. Find out more about them here!
